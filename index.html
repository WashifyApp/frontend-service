Frequently Asked Questions

How does the virtual queue work?

When you book a service, you receive a queue number. You can track the current queue status in real-time from your dashboard and arrive right before your turn.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking from your dashboard at any time before the wash starts.

Are the cleaning products safe for ceramic coatings?

Absolutely. All Washify partners are required to use pH-neutral, safe products that protect and enhance existing coatings.
